• Privacy Policy

     

    Last Updated: September 2025

    Effective Date: September 2025

    Thank you for using the HiFier app! We highly value your privacy and the protection of your personal information. This Privacy Policy is intended to clearly and transparently explain how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your data.

    Please read and understand this Privacy Policy carefully before using the HiFier app. If you do not agree with any part of this Policy, please discontinue using our app.

     

    1. How We Collect Data

    1. Application Itself

      • HiFier does not actively collect your personal information, nor do we actively collect device hardware, operating system details, contacts, or similar data.

    2. User-Provided Information

      • When using the app, you may provide account authorization (e.g., mounted cloud drives, media server login credentials). Such information is stored locally on your device only. We do not upload, retain, or share this information.

    3. Third-Party SDKs

      • To ensure stable operation, deliver ads, and perform analytics, we integrate third-party SDKs. These SDKs may collect device-related data (see Third-Party SDK Privacy).

     

    2. How We Use and Store Your Data

    1. Authorization Information

      • Cloud drive and media server credentials are stored locally on your device. When you remove the service or uninstall the app, this information is permanently deleted and not retained on any of our servers.

    2. Files and Data

      • The app only accesses your mounted cloud drives, media servers, or imported files in a read-only manner.

      • We do not modify, upload, or share your files.

    3. Downloaded Files

      • Files you download within the app are stored locally for browsing and playback only.

      • You may delete them manually, and they will also be deleted upon app uninstallation. Once deleted, they cannot be recovered.

    4. Playback Cache

      • Cache files generated during playback are used solely to improve playback performance.

      • These files are not uploaded. You may clear them in app settings, and they will also be deleted upon app uninstallation.

    5. Playback Progress

      • If you log in to a media server, we will report your playback progress to that server to record your viewing history.

      • This feature is currently always enabled and cannot be turned off.

    6. Lyrics and Cover Art Search

      • When enabled (default setting), the app may send the song title, artist name, album name, or filename to third-party search services to retrieve lyrics and cover art.

      • Results are stored locally only and never uploaded.

      • You may disable this feature in the settings.

    7. Music Recognition

      • We use Apple’s system services to provide music recognition features.

      • For related privacy policies, please refer to:

        • Shazam Privacy Policy

        • Apple Privacy Policy

     

    3. Third-Party SDK Privacy

    1. Umeng Analytics (Umeng SDK)

       

      • Purpose: Crash analytics and usage statistics to improve user experience.

      • Data that may be collected: IMEI/MAC/Android ID/IDFA/OAID/OpenUDID/GUID/SIM IMSI/ICCID, location information, network information, etc.

      • Privacy Policy: https://www.umeng.com/page/policy

    2. Advertising Platforms

      • Non-subscribed users will see ads. Once you subscribe, no advertising platforms will be initialized at the next app startup.

      • Mainland China users: Ads are provided by Pangle (穿山甲). Privacy Policy: https://www.csjplatform.com/privacy/partner

      • Non-Mainland China users: Ads are provided by AppLovin and Google.

        • AppLovin Privacy Policy

        • Google Privacy Policy

    4. Purposes of Data Usage

    We collect or process data solely for the following purposes:

    1. To provide core app features (file playback, caching, etc.).

    2. To fix bugs, improve stability, and enhance user experience.

    3. To analyze crash reports and improve product quality.

    4. To enable personalized advertising.

    5. Data Security Measures

     

    1. Local Storage

      • Authorization information, cache, and downloaded files are stored only on your device and are never uploaded.

    2. Secure Transmission

      • Third-party SDKs use encrypted protocols (e.g., HTTPS/TLS) for data transmission.

    3. Internal Management

      • We do not maintain centralized databases and do not store personal information on our servers.

    6. Data Retention and Deletion

    1. Local Data: Deleted when you remove the related service or uninstall the app.

    2. Third-Party Data: Managed independently by SDK providers. Retention periods depend on their policies.

    3. Deletion Requests: You may contact us to request the deletion of data collected by third-party SDKs.

     

     

    7. Your Rights

    Depending on applicable laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA, China’s Personal Information Protection Law), you may exercise the following rights:

    1. Right to Know: Understand how your data is collected, used, and shared.

    2. Right of Access: Request access to your personal data we process.

    3. Right of Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

    4. Right of Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal limitations).

    5. Right to Restrict Processing: Request restriction of how your data is processed.

    6. Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

    7. Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent to certain data processing activities where applicable.

     

    8. Protection of Minors

    • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 14.

    • If a parent or guardian believes that we have unintentionally collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately, and we will promptly delete the information.

     

    9. International Users and Cross-Border Data Transfers

    • The HiFier app may be used by users in different regions worldwide.

    • Third-party SDKs (such as Google and AppLovin) may transfer data to servers outside your country, including the United States.

    • By using the app, you consent to such cross-border data transfers.

     

    10. Updates to This Privacy Policy

    • We may revise this Privacy Policy in response to business adjustments, regulatory requirements, or other reasons.

    • The updated version will be displayed within the app, with the latest “Last Updated” date indicated.

    • For material changes, we will provide prominent notice within the app.
